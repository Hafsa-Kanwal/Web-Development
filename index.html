    • Introduction

    My name is Hafsa Kanwal, and I am 20 years old. I hail from the beautiful city of Rawalakot, but currently, I reside in Mirpur to pursue my studies. My father is a dedicated teacher, and I come from a large, loving family with six siblings. Growing up in a family where education is highly valued, I have always been passionate about learning and exploring new fields.

    My Education

    I have recently completed my graduation in Software Engineering and am eager to continue expanding my knowledge, particularly in web design and development. Aside from my academic pursuits, I enjoy spending time with my family, engaging in creative hobbies, and staying updated with the latest trends in technology.

Designing websites using WordPress is something I truly enjoy, as it allows me to combine creativity with technical expertise. Additionally, I love reading books, which not only helps me relax but also broadens my perspective and knowledge in various areas. These hobbies keep me engaged, constantly learning, and motivated to achieve my goals.
